A dual solution

HowCrowded provides a dual solution to make it easier both for people and organizations to keep social distance.

This is achieved by:

  • Sensors provided to indoor locations accessible by the public such as grocery stores, libraries or gyms.
  • An app/website that the public can use to check both real time how many people are in an indoor location, and also general trends so they can plan their visit when there is less crowded.
  • Technology that can be used by organizations to manage information about how many people are in their store at a given time. This can be used for planning and organization as well as setting up a screen at the front door to control the people going in and out.


Consumer

This will make it much easier for people to plan their trips to public indoor spaces as they can both check in real-time how many people and see how many people that usually visit at given times.

Organization

For organizations, they no longer have to count manually how many people are in the location, and for smaller stores this could mean that they will no longer need to use a member of staff to control the door as the customer is told by the screen when it is ready for them to go into the space.

Differentiating us from our competitors

There are similar concepts to the product we are offering. Google for example has a function that shows how busy a place is, and also does forecasts for different times of the day. Furthermore, the technology for using sensors to track people going in and out of a store also exicitis, however the practice of using these are not widespread. However, the genius lies in combining these two services. The stores will better manage the flow of customers, because they give the real time information to them, and the customers will get an accurate number of how many people are in the store at a given time - making it easier for them to plan when they are going to a certain location.
