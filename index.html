Challenge


During the course of the Covid-19 pandemic, social distancing has been key in helping to stop the spread of the virus. Shopping malls, stores and gyms are often crowded with people at the same time. This creates a challenge in maintaining safe social distancing.

Solution


Our app is designed to make social distancing easier. HowCrowded shows you the optimal times to visit malls, stores and places to maintain a safe distance from others to reduce the spread of COVID-19.
